№ 05 — How we work

From first conversation to continuity.

A defined five-step path. Each phase has its own deliverables and its own commitments. You see the architecture before the pitch — the only way to know whether you're working with infrastructure or improvisation.

i.

Intake

A structured form and a 30-minute conversation. We assess fit before scope, scope before pricing.

15-min form · 30-min call
ii.

Discovery

Stakeholders, context, constraints. We surface the variables that determine whether the program is feasible at the level promised.

5–10 business days
iii.

Architecture

A documented proposal with supplier validation summary, risk contingencies, and clear cost structure. No filler.

10–14 business days
iv.

Execution

The program runs on validated suppliers and documented processes. Global Rescue, medical evacuation, and risk-advisory protocols active throughout. Itinerary delivered through the Fora Travel app with offline PDF backup. Single point of contact, end to end.

Continuity

№ 07 — Who it's for

The studio is selective by design.

A mismatch creates the kind of friction the brand is built to eliminate. We're direct about fit because it protects you and us.

We work well with

  • Clients who treat travel as a decision, not a transaction
  • Family principals coordinating complex group logistics
  • Operations leads briefing corporate or MICE programs
  • Repeat travelers building a documented program over years
  • Stakeholders who value validated suppliers over name-dropping

We're not the right studio for

  • Last-minute trip requests with no discovery phase
  • Single-component bookings — hotels only, flights only
  • Clients seeking influencer-style content or staged moments
  • Projects under budget thresholds that prevent operational rigor
  • Engagements that bypass the structured intake
